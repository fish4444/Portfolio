UI/UX: Responsive Redesign

ResearchData AnalyticsUX

Overview

During an injury that I acquired I was looking around for an x-ray and came across this site. It is terribly designed and I decided to add responsive re-design to it.

Problem

This is the home page of the x-ray product company. I saw its terrible design and decided to redesign it. I wanted to make this website usable for people who need x-ray parts and x-rays.

Sketches

I created sketches of my initial designs. My idea was to change the style but keep the functionality. I removed the color pallet to a more softer but colorful style. I added a navigation bar for easy motion around the site. I decided to keep the popular product image as to keep some of the functionality of the site and structure. The addition of the buttons add functionality, hierarchy, and style to the page. The buttons are the most popular searches. For the mobile version I wanted to keep the hierarchy so I kept the navigation bar and the buttons but in a vertical mobile style .

Re-design / Solution

The website includes the dynamic and updated version of the original website. By implementing the Figma style I re-designed a responsive website.
